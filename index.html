🥞 Pancake Recipe

This easy homemade pancake recipe gives you soft, fluffy, and tasty pancakes in just a few minutes. Perfect for breakfast, snacks, or even a small food business idea.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk or water
  • 2–3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon oil or melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder (optional)
  • Pinch of salt and vanilla (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix all dry ingredients first.
  2. Add wet ingredients and stir until smooth.
  3. Heat pan and grease lightly.
  4. Pour batter and cook until bubbles appear.
  5. Flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
